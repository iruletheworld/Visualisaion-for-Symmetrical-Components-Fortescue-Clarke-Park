cal_samples()¶

This method calculates the samples needed for plotting.

The sampling rate is 6 times the maximum frequency.

Parameters:

self
Returns:

samples : float

The number of samples.

Examples

samples = self.cal_samples()

array_time = np.linspace(0, self.timeEnd, samples)
make_phase(mag, omega, phi, dc)¶

This method creates the phase signal in complex form.

Parameters:

self

mag : float

The magnitude of the signal.

omega : float

The angular frequency of the signal.

phi : float

The initial phase of the signal.

dc : float

The DC offset of the signal.
Returns:

complex

The signal in complex form.

array_time : list

The sampled time.

Examples

self.phaseAdata, self.time_samples = self.make_phase(self.phaseAMag,
                                                     self.phaseAOmega,
                                                     self.phaseAPhi,
                                                     self.phaseADC)
