9. Biography¶

GAO, Siyu received the Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering and its Automation from the North China Electric Power University, China in 2010. He also received an B.Eng (Hons) degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Manchester, UK in 2010 (First Class with Honours). He then received a Ph.D degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Manchester, UK in 2014.

He then joined the Belgium grid opertor, Elia, to work on VSC-HVDC projects and worked closely with RTE, REE, 50 Hz, ABB, Siemens, Alstom/GE.

From early 2017, he has joined GE to develop next generation VSC-HVDC MMC.

His area of interest is VSC-HVDC, modelling, control, computer science, programming, literature, art and human behaviour.
